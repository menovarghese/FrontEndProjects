About Church


Nilackal Orthodox Church is one of the prominent churches of the Malankara Diocese. The church, which is in the holy intercession of twelve apostles, is a sanctuary for thousands of families in this region. The commemorative feast of the twelve apostles is a major event conducted in the Nilackal Orthodox Church. The festival usually falls in the month of June, at a time when monsoons reach Godâ€™s Own Country. Thousands of believers in and around Nilackal attend the special prayers conducted in the church during the festival days. Spectacular fireworks are a major attraction of the feast. The festivities conclude with a colourful procession.
